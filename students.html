Students Corner

AikyaNova partners with emerging scientists, engineers, and designers to accelerate ideas that advance bio-intelligent healthcare. Explore programs, mentorship, and real-world challenges tailored for student innovators.

Innovation residencies

Join focused 8–12 week residencies where student teams co-build prototypes with our R&D mentors across molecular systems, intelligent sensing, and human-centered design.

Capstone collaborations

Translate classroom research into actionable impact by aligning final-year and graduate capstones with live problem statements from our translational pipelines.

Open innovation challenges

Compete in themed sprints that pair biology, data, and systems engineering to unlock solutions for diagnostics, smart therapeutics, and care delivery.
